ABOUT US

Izintombu Zodwa Trading

Izintombu Zodwa Trading is a proud 100% Black female-owned company specializing in the manufacturing and distribution of Personal Protective Equipment (PPE), Corporate Wear, and School Uniforms. Our company was established as part of Actom Pty Ltd's B-BBEE initiative, with the purpose of introducing a Black-owned enterprise into the PPE supply chain. Our mission extends beyond this, as we also aim to create opportunities for our beneficiaries to thrive and access new markets.

As a Level 01 B-BBEE compliant enterprise, Izintombu Zodwa Trading is your trusted designated supplier. We ensure +162% recognition for our clients against their procurement spend. Our commitment to excellence and empowerment drives us to deliver top-quality products while making a positive impact on the community and the business landscape.
